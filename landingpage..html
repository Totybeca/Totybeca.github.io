How Patient Record System came about

Late last year I was sick, and so I went to the Lagos university teaching hospital for treatment. I discovered that the process required to get a patient's card was rigorous and exhausting as they had piles of card with registration numbers writtenon them. There were so many people waiting for the clerk to look for their card before they could see a doctor, and those that were coming for the first time like me were asked to wait first.It took the whole day before about 200 people’s cards were found and the others were asked to cme in another time. I ended up not seeing a doctor and i was given an appointment for another 3 months. So I thought of a better way to make this easy and fast. And that was where the idea of patient record system came in.
